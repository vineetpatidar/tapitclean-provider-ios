What personal data do we collect and why do we collect it

Information about providers
We collect the data that our providers submit to us: Business Name Business Phone E-mail Business Description Parking address with zip code* Website Photos of vehicles Also we collect IP addresses from which that information was sent.

Geo-location data of our providers
When providers send us their geo-location data we collect only the last one for 1 hour. We use that geo-location for visitors to be able to view the drivers that are close to their location. So The MrKnowItAllTowing Service offers publicly accessible information about the provider’s geo-location data.

Reviews
When visitors leave reviews on MrKnowItAllTowing Service we collect the data shown in the feedback forms, and also the visitor’s IP address and browser user agent string to help spam detection.
